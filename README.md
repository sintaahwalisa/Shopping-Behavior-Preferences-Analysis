# Shopping Behavior & Preferences Analysis

This project analyzes **customer shopping behavior and preferences** to understand how people make purchasing decisions in a retail setting. The goal is to find patterns that can help improve **product offerings, pricing, and customer experience**.

---

## What This Project Is About

Customers do not all shop the same way. Some prioritize price, others prefer certain brands or categories. This project looks at **transactional and behavioral data** to understand how customers shop and what they prefer.

---

## Why This Matters

Understanding shopping behavior helps retailers:

* Offer the right products
* Design better promotions
* Improve customer satisfaction
* Make data-driven merchandising decisions

---

## What I Analyzed

The analysis focuses on:

* Purchase frequency and spending patterns
* Product and category preferences
* Differences in behavior across customer groups
* Signals that explain buying choices

---

## How the Analysis Was Done

* Cleaned and prepared shopping data
* Explored customer and transaction patterns
* Compared preferences across segments
* Summarized insights in a clear and practical way

---

## Key Outputs

* Insights into customer shopping preferences
* Behavioral patterns across different customer groups
* Findings that support retail decision-making

---

## Tools Used

* Data analysis and visualization tools
* Structured exploratory data analysis (EDA)

---

## Outcome

The project provides a clearer view of **how customers shop and what drives their preferences**, helping retailers make better decisions around **assortment, pricing, and promotions**.
